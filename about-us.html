ABOUT US 

Paw Society is a non-profit organization that gains its financial asset through the help of different people from different places who support our advocacy for animal care and shelter. As a non- profit organization, we don't benefit even a single peso from this campaign. All donations and money we get from the kind hearts of the people go directly to the funding and maintenance of the facility where we are taking care of the sheltered animals. Through the determination and willingness of all the members of our team, staffs and the people who have a big heart regarding our cause, we can definitely achieve our goals in making a better society between man and animals.
